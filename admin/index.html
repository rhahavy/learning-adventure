📚 Curriculum Editor —

Edit the grade-level master curriculum — one canonical lesson set per grade (JK – G8). Saved overrides apply to every tenant on next page load, and the runtime fallback in the app makes them flow to every same-grade student automatically (so editing Grade 3 here ships to every G3 kid in every tenant). Per-tenant teacher edits still win on top, so a tutor's local customizations aren't undone.

🎯 Editing the grade master. Per-student edits (IEP scaffolds, ESL simplifications, hiding a lesson for one kid) belong on the per-tenant Teacher Dashboard, not here — those overrides layer on top of the grade master without affecting siblings or classmates.
