Adapts to each kid

Two engines watching every answer

Solvix nudges struggling kids gently down — and challenges flying kids up — without you lifting a finger.

🌉
Early Bridge
Catches kids before they spiral

The moment a kid misses 3 of the last 5 questions, Solvix swaps the next one for something easier — or pulls Nova in to walk it through step-by-step. No “ask your teacher” dead-ends.

  • Triggers automatically — no parent or teacher setup
  • Adjusts faster for kids on an IEP plan (2 of last 3)
  • Resets the moment they’re back on track
🚀
Stretch Mode
Pushes fast kids past easy mastery

Once a kid aces an activity, the next visit serves harder questions in the same skill — not a copy-paste of what they already mastered. If they slip on the harder version, it backs off automatically.

  • Per-skill, lifetime-tracked progress
  • Backs off if recent accuracy drops below 60%
  • Stays on grade-level curriculum, not random extension

Questions

Things parents & teachers ask

Does my child need to download anything?

No. Solvix runs in any modern browser — iPad, Chromebook, phone, or laptop. Once loaded, it even works offline.

What ages is Solvix for?

Designed for elementary learners, roughly ages 4–14 (junior kindergarten through grade 8). Content adapts to each child's actual level, not just their grade.

How do you protect my child's data?

We never sell student data — ever. Each classroom's data lives in its own private vault, accessible only with your classroom PIN. Delete your classroom anytime and the data is wiped.

Is the free trial really free?

Yes — 7 days, all features, on any plan. A card is required at signup so your kids don't lose their streak if you forget to subscribe, but you can cancel in one click before day 7 and won't be charged.

Can I switch between Family and Classroom plans?

Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime from your billing portal. Progress transfers automatically.

Do yearly plans really save that much?

Yep. Family goes from $120/yr (monthly) down to $79/yr (~34% off). Classroom goes from $360/yr down to $249/yr (~31% off). Same features either way. The launch promo (50% off) stacks on top of either plan, so the yearly price you see on the card is already discounted.

Is the 50% off promo a trick — does it expire?

It's a real launch discount, applied automatically at checkout — no code to enter. Right now we're offering it to everyone who signs up during the launch window. If you sign up while the promo is live, the discount stays on your subscription through Stripe (no surprise full-price renewal). If you don't see the strike-through price on the cards above, the promo has ended.
