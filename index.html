Questions

Things parents & teachers ask

Does my child need to download anything?

No. KidQuest runs in any modern browser — iPad, Chromebook, phone, or laptop. Once loaded, it even works offline.

What ages is KidQuest for?

Designed for elementary learners, roughly ages 5–12 (kindergarten through grade 6). Content adapts to each child's actual level, not just their grade.

How do you protect my child's data?

We never sell student data — ever. Each classroom's data lives in its own private vault, accessible only with your classroom PIN. Delete your classroom anytime and the data is wiped.

Is the free trial really free?

Yes — 7 days, all features, on any plan. A card is required at signup so your kids don't lose their streak if you forget to subscribe, but you can cancel in one click before day 7 and won't be charged.

Can I switch between Family and Classroom plans?

Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime from your billing portal. Progress transfers automatically.

Do yearly plans really save that much?

Yep. Family goes from $120/yr (monthly) down to $79/yr (~34% off). Classroom goes from $360/yr down to $249/yr (~31% off). Same features either way.
